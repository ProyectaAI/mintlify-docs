Proyecta is built to meet enterprise security, privacy, compliance, and operational requirements. Whether you need a custom usage agreement, dedicated support, or compliance guarantees, the Enterprise plan is designed around your organization's needs.

To get started, reach out to [sales@proyecta.dev](mailto:sales@proyecta.dev).

## What Enterprise includes

| Requirement | How Proyecta supports it |
|-------------|--------------------------|
| **Identity and access management** | OAuth (GitHub, Google, Microsoft); role-based access controls; workspace-level permissions |
| **Security** | Encryption in transit and at rest; isolated runtime environments; container-based tenant isolation |
| **Privacy** | No customer data used for AI training; data ownership guarantees |
| **Scalability** | Proyecta Cloud infrastructure; auto-scaling runtime pods |
| **Reliability** | Cloud-native architecture; health monitoring and auto-recovery |
| **Support** | Dedicated support; onboarding assistance |
| **Integrations** | GitHub integration; API access; webhook support |
| **Customization** | Custom templates; branded experiences; configurable environments |
| **Business continuity** | Code stored in your GitHub repository; database export available |

## Planned enterprise features

These capabilities are in development and will be available to Enterprise customers:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="SAML SSO">
    Sign in to Proyecta through your corporate identity provider — Okta, Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD), Google Workspace, or any SAML 2.0 / OIDC-compatible IdP.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SCIM provisioning">
    Automatically create and deprovision Proyecta accounts when employees join or leave, synced directly from your IdP.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SOC 2 compliance">
    SOC 2 Type II certification is in progress. Contact sales for the current status and to request a copy of reports.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="HIPAA compliance">
    A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) and HIPAA-aligned configuration for healthcare organizations handling protected health information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data residency">
    Choose your hosting region — US or EU — to meet data sovereignty requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Audit logs">
    Comprehensive activity tracking across your organization: who did what, when, across all projects and settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Note>
  If you need any of these capabilities today, [contact sales](/company/contact) to discuss your timeline and whether an early-access arrangement is possible.
</Note>
