Your Proyecta workspace is built for teams. Invite colleagues to collaborate on apps, assign the right level of access to each person, and work together from a shared project environment — all without leaving the builder.

## Invite a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Navigate to the **Settings** page from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the Team section">
    Select the **Team** section in the left panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email address">
    Type the email address of the person you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. Your teammate receives an email with a link to join your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

Each team member has one of three roles that controls what they can do inside the workspace.

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Member** | View and edit apps |
| **Admin** | Everything a Member can do, plus manage team settings |
| **Owner** | Everything an Admin can do, plus manage billing |

<Tip>
  Assign the **Admin** role to colleagues who need to onboard new teammates or change workspace settings. Reserve **Owner** for the person responsible for the subscription.
</Tip>

## How collaboration works

Once a teammate accepts your invitation, they share access to all projects in the workspace. Each team member can:

- Open any project in the builder
- Chat with the AI to make changes
- View and edit code directly
- Publish updates to the live app

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can teammates work on the same project at the same time?">
    Each builder session operates on the project's shared codebase. Multiple teammates can work on the same project, but editing at the same time may cause conflicts. Use thread history and coordination through branches to avoid collisions — see [Version Control](/apps/version-control) for branch workflows.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many teammates can I have?">
    The number of seats depends on your plan. Free plans have limited seats, while Pro and Business plans support more team members. See [Billing & Credits](/platform/billing-and-credits) for plan details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a person belong to multiple workspaces?">
    Yes. A single Proyecta account can belong to multiple workspaces. Users switch between workspaces from the workspace selector.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
