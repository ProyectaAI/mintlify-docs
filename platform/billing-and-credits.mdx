Every Proyecta subscription bundles hosting, AI credits, and access to platform features in a single plan. You manage everything — plan changes, credit top-ups, invoices — from **Settings > Billing**.

## Plans

Proyecta offers four tiers to match where you are:

| Plan | What's included |
|------|-----------------|
| **Free** | Limited AI credits, free `*.proyecta.dev` subdomain |
| **Pro** | More AI credits, custom domains, priority support |
| **Business** | Higher limits, team collaboration, advanced features |
| **Enterprise** | Custom limits, dedicated support, SLA |

<Note>
  For Enterprise pricing and requirements, see [Enterprise](/platform/enterprise) or contact [sales@proyecta.dev](mailto:sales@proyecta.dev).
</Note>

## AI credits

AI credits are consumed whenever the AI agent writes code, answers questions, or makes changes to your app. Different operations use different amounts — generating a complex feature costs more credits than a simple text edit.

### View your usage

Go to **Settings > Billing** to see:

- Your current AI credit balance
- Usage history
- Current plan details
- Invoice history

## Upgrading your plan

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a new plan">
    Select the plan you want to upgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm payment">
    Review the prorated charge and confirm. Credits are granted immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

When you upgrade, you pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your current billing cycle. Your billing cycle stays anchored to your original signup date.

## Downgrading your plan

Downgrades take effect at the **start of your next billing cycle**. You keep all the features of your current plan until then. To downgrade, open **Settings > Billing** and select a lower plan.

## Getting more credits

If you run out of AI credits before your cycle renews, you can:

- **Upgrade your plan** for a higher monthly credit allowance
- **Purchase additional credits** from the Billing settings page

<Warning>
  If you run out of credits, your published app stays live but you won't be able to use the AI agent until you add more credits or your next billing cycle begins.
</Warning>

## Cancelling

To cancel your subscription, go to **Settings > Billing** and select the Free plan. Your account moves to Free at the start of your next billing cycle — you keep paid features until then.
