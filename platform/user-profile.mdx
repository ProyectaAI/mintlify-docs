---
title: "Manage Your Proyecta User Profile and Settings"
description: "Update your display name, email, password, preferred language, and light/dark theme from the Proyecta Settings page — changes apply immediately."
---

Your profile and preferences live in the **Settings** page, which you can open from the navigation at any time. From there you can update your display name, change your email, reset your password, switch languages, and toggle between light and dark mode.

## Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your avatar or navigate to the **Settings** page from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your name or email">
    In the **Profile** section, update your first name, last name, or email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply the updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Security settings">
    Open **Settings** and select the **Security** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request a password reset">
    Click **Reset Password**. Proyecta sends an email to your registered address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your new password">
    Follow the link in the email to choose and confirm a new password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you sign in with Google or Microsoft OAuth, you manage your password through that provider — the Reset Password option applies to email/password accounts.
</Note>

## Language

Proyecta supports **English** and **Spanish**. Switch your preferred language from the **Settings** page or by using the language selector in the interface. The change takes effect immediately.

## Theme

Toggle between **light mode** and **dark mode** using the theme switcher in the **Settings** page. Your preference is saved to your account and persists across sessions.
