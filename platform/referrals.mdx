A formal referral program — where you earn credits when someone you refer signs up for a paid plan — is planned for a future release. There is no shareable tracking link or referrals dashboard today.

## What you can do right now

While the program is in development, you can still recommend Proyecta to friends and colleagues in a few practical ways:

- **Share the homepage** — send anyone to `https://proyecta.dev` to explore and sign up on their own.
- **Share a published app** you've built — your `*.proyecta.dev` subdomain or custom domain works as a live demo of what Proyecta can produce.
- **Drop a template link** — when creating a project, you can point someone to a template so they start from a known-good foundation rather than a blank slate.

## What's coming

The planned referral program will include:

- **Shareable referral links** — a unique link tied to your account that you can share anywhere.
- **Credits for paid sign-ups** — earn AI credits whenever a referred user upgrades to a paid plan.
- **Referrals dashboard** — track pending and confirmed referrals in one place.
- **No referral cap** — refer as many people as you like, with perks for power referrers.
