Every project comes with a real backend, database, authentication, and a GitHub repository you own.

## How Proyecta works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your app">
    Type a prompt — "Build a task manager with kanban boards" — or pick a template. Proyecta's AI reads your request, writes the code, and opens a live preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refine with chat">
    Use the AI chat to add features, fix bugs, or change the design. Every update appears instantly in the live preview. All changes commit automatically to your GitHub repo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish in one click">
    Click **Publish** to deploy to a free `yourapp.proyecta.dev` subdomain. Bring your own domain anytime from the **Domains** panel.
  </Step>
</Steps>
