Proyecta enables everyone to build custom software by describing what they want in plain language. You don't need to be a developer to ship a production app — you just need an idea and a conversation.

## Our mission

> "Our mission is to empower anyone to build production-ready software through natural conversation with AI."

## What we do

Proyecta makes it easy to build full-stack apps and websites by chatting with AI. Describe what you want and the AI writes production-grade code, sets up databases, configures infrastructure, and deploys your app — all in real time.

You can build SaaS platforms, marketplaces, internal tools, dashboards, and more — going from idea to a live, publicly accessible app in minutes.
