Proyecta turns a plain description into a working app. You describe what you want, the AI writes the code, and a live preview updates as it builds. When you're satisfied, publish to a free subdomain or your own domain in one click. This guide walks you through that full flow from start to finish.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Sign in to [Proyecta](https://proyecta.dev) and click **Start Building** from the dashboard. You have three ways to begin:

    - **Describe your app** — type a prompt like "Build a task management app with kanban boards"
    - **Pick a template** — choose from pre-built templates to skip the blank-canvas stage
    - **Import from GitHub** — connect an existing repository to bring your codebase into the builder
  </Step>

  <Step title="Refine with AI chat">
    Once your project is created, Proyecta provisions a development environment and opens the builder. Use the **AI chat panel** on the left to describe features, fix bugs, or make changes. The live preview on the right updates in real time as the AI edits your code.

    <Tip>
      Start a fresh chat thread for each new task. Shorter, focused conversations produce more accurate results. See [AI Accuracy Tips](/ai/improve-accuracy) for more.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish your app">
    Click the **Publish** button in the toolbar. Choose a free `yourapp.proyecta.dev` subdomain or connect a custom domain. Your app goes live in seconds.

    <Note>
      Every change you make is committed to your GitHub repository automatically. Re-publishing after further edits takes one click.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
