Every Proyecta app ships with a free `yourapp.proyecta.dev` subdomain, and you can connect a custom domain you own at any time. Proyecta handles TLS certificate provisioning automatically — once your DNS records are in place, your app is served over HTTPS with no extra steps.

<Note>
  Buying or registering new domains directly inside Proyecta is not yet supported. Purchase your domain from any registrar — Namecheap, GoDaddy, Cloudflare, Google Domains, or others — then connect it using the steps below.
</Note>

## Connect a custom domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Publish dialog">
    Click **Publish** in the builder toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Custom Domain">
    Select **Custom Domain** and enter your domain name (for example, `myapp.com`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the DNS records">
    Proyecta displays the DNS records you need to add. Go to your domain registrar's DNS management panel and add the records shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for propagation and TLS provisioning">
    DNS changes can take anywhere from a few minutes to 48 hours to propagate. Proyecta provisions your SSL certificate automatically once your records are verified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Required DNS records

The exact values are displayed in the Publish dialog after you enter your domain. The record types you'll need are:

| Type | Name | Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| A | `@` | Provided during setup |
| CNAME | `www` | Provided during setup |

Add both records to cover both the root domain and the `www` subdomain.

## Domain status

After adding your records, you can track progress in the Publish dialog:

| Status | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Pending Verification** | Waiting for your DNS records to be configured |
| **Verified** | DNS records confirmed |
| **Provisioning TLS** | SSL certificate is being set up |
| **Active** | Your domain is live and serving your app |
| **Failed** | Something went wrong — double-check your DNS config |

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple domains to the same app?">
    Yes. You can add multiple custom domains from the Publish dialog. All domains serve the same app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does DNS propagation take?">
    Usually minutes, but it can take up to 48 hours depending on your DNS provider and TTL settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is HTTPS included?">
    Yes. SSL/TLS certificates are provisioned automatically for all custom domains at no extra cost.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I remove a domain?">
    Open the Publish dialog and delete the domain from the list.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
