If you already own a domain — whether it's registered with Namecheap, GoDaddy, Cloudflare, or any other registrar — you can connect it to your Proyecta app in a few minutes. All you need to do is add two DNS records at your registrar and Proyecta handles the rest, including provisioning your SSL certificate.

## Connect your domain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Publish dialog">
    Click **Publish** in the builder toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Custom Domain">
    Choose **Custom Domain** and enter your domain name, for example `myapp.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the DNS records">
    Proyecta displays the A record and CNAME record values you need to add. Keep this dialog open while you go to your registrar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add records at your registrar">
    Log in to your domain registrar's DNS management panel and add the records shown in the Publish dialog.

    | Type | Name | Value |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | A | `@` | Provided in Publish dialog |
    | CNAME | `www` | Provided in Publish dialog |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for propagation">
    Return to the Publish dialog to monitor your domain's status. DNS changes typically take a few minutes but can take up to 48 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What are DNS records?

DNS records tell the internet where to find your website. When someone types your domain into a browser, their device looks up the DNS records for that domain to find the server to connect to. The A record points your root domain (`myapp.com`) to Proyecta's servers, and the CNAME record does the same for `www.myapp.com`.

## Troubleshooting

If your domain isn't working after you've added the records:

1. Verify the records are saved correctly at your registrar — it's easy to accidentally add a trailing dot or extra character
2. Wait up to 48 hours for DNS propagation to complete globally
3. Check the domain status in the Publish dialog
4. If the status shows **Failed**, re-check your DNS configuration and contact support if the problem persists

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Will www work as well as the root domain?">
    Yes. The A record covers the root domain (`myapp.com`) and the CNAME covers `www.myapp.com`. Add both records to ensure both work.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect multiple domains to the same app?">
    Yes. You can connect as many domains as you like to a single app from the Publish dialog.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long until my domain starts working?">
    Usually within minutes, but DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to fully propagate worldwide.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My domain works on one device but not another. What's happening?">
    This is usually a DNS caching issue. Try clearing your browser cache, flushing your local DNS resolver, or simply waiting a few more hours for propagation to complete.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use a free subdomain instead?">
    Yes. All apps include a free `yourapp.proyecta.dev` subdomain with no DNS setup required. See [Free Subdomain](/apps/publish/proyecta-domain).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
