Every Proyecta app can be published to a free subdomain at `yourapp.proyecta.dev`, included with all plans. It takes seconds to set up — no DNS configuration, no certificates to manage. When you're ready for a branded URL, you can connect a custom domain at any time without breaking anything.

## Publish to your free subdomain

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Publish dialog">
    Click **Publish** in the builder toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your subdomain name">
    Type the subdomain you want, for example `my-cool-app`. Your app will be live at `my-cool-app.proyecta.dev`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Publish">
    Your app is live immediately at the URL you chose.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Subdomain rules

Subdomains must meet these requirements:

- 3–63 characters
- Lowercase letters, numbers, and hyphens only
- Must start and end with a letter or number
- No consecutive hyphens
- Cannot be purely numeric

## Reserved subdomains

The following names are reserved and cannot be claimed: `www`, `api`, `app`, `admin`, `mail`, `dashboard`, `login`, `signup`, `auth`, `static`, `assets`, `cdn`, `docs`, `help`, `support`, `status`, `blog`, `billing`, `account`, `settings`.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my subdomain?">
    Yes. Open the Publish dialog and update your subdomain name. The old URL will stop working as soon as you save the change, so update any links you've shared.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many subdomains do I get?">
    One free `proyecta.dev` subdomain per app.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I publish to my own domain instead?">
    Connect a domain you already own by following the steps in [Connect an Existing Domain](/apps/publish/connect-existing-domain).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
