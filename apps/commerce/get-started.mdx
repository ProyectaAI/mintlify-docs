Proyecta Commerce gives every app a full payment system without leaving the builder. Connect Stripe once, define what you sell, and start accepting payments — subscriptions, one-time charges, or free plans — all managed from the **Commerce** panel in the builder toolbar.

<Note>
**Beta.** The core flows (Stripe Connect onboarding, products, customers, transactions, coupons) are live. Some advanced settings are still being filled in.
</Note>

## What's in the Commerce panel

The Commerce panel has five tabs, each covering a different part of your payment system:

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Products** | Define what you sell (subscriptions, one-time, free) |
| **Customers** | The billable users of your app |
| **Transactions** | Successful charges, refunds, and disputes |
| **Coupons** | Discount codes customers can apply at checkout |
| **Settings** | Connect your Stripe account (Payment Entity) |

## Get set up in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    Commerce uses Stripe Connect under the hood. You don't need an existing Stripe account — you'll create one through Stripe's hosted onboarding flow.

    1. Open the **Commerce** panel and switch to the **Settings** tab
    2. Click **Connect Stripe**
    3. Enter your **Business name** and pick your **Country**
    4. Click **Create Account**
    5. Click **Complete Setup** to open Stripe's hosted onboarding in a new tab
    6. Fill in your business details, banking information, and verification documents
    7. When you return, your Payment Entity status moves from `onboarding` → `active`

    <Note>
    The connect flow currently supports: United States · United Kingdom · Canada · Australia · Germany · France · Spain · Italy · Netherlands · Japan · Singapore · Brazil · Mexico · India · Sweden. If your country isn't listed, [contact support](/company/contact).
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first product">
    Once your Payment Entity is `active`, switch to the **Products** tab and click **Create product**. Every product needs at least one **variant** — a pricing tier — with a price, currency, and billing type (one-time or recurring).

    You can also ask the AI to do it for you:

    ```text
    Create a Pro plan at $29/month and a Business plan at $99/month.
    ```

    See [Products & Features](/apps/commerce/products-features) for the full options, including how to attach entitlements to products.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wire checkout into your app">
    Use the Proyecta SDK from your app code to start a checkout session and check feature access. See [Integrate Into My App](/apps/commerce/integrate-into-my-app) for complete code samples.
  </Step>
</Steps>
