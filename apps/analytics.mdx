Every Proyecta app ships with analytics enabled automatically. There is nothing to install, no snippet to paste, and no third-party account required. Open the **Analytics** panel in the builder to view data from your published site.

## The Analytics panel

The Analytics panel has three tabs along the top:

| Tab | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Overview** | Traffic — page views, visitors, top pages, referrers, and geography |
| **Web Vitals** | Core Web Vitals (LCP, INP, CLS) captured from real users |
| **Devices** | Browser, operating system, and device-type breakdowns |

## Accessing your analytics

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Open your project in the Proyecta builder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to the Analytics panel">
    Click the **Analytics** tab in the builder toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the data">
    The Overview tab loads by default. Use the tab bar at the top to jump between Overview, Web Vitals, and Devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You'll see a spinner while the runtime comes online, then the data appears.

## What data is tracked

Analytics are collected automatically from your **published** site — your `*.proyecta.dev` subdomain or your custom domain. Preview traffic inside the builder is not counted.

The current window is the **last 7 days**. A configurable date range picker is on the roadmap.

<Note>
  Proyecta analytics do not set cookies or track users across sites. You do not need a cookie banner for analytics alone.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to add Google Analytics or Mixpanel?">
    No. Analytics are built in and enabled automatically for every published app. If you want a third-party provider in addition to built-in analytics, you can still ask the AI to integrate one — add your provider's API key in Environment Variables and say `"Add Google Analytics to my app"`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see any data?">
    Analytics only track the **published** version of your app. If you haven't published yet, or if your published site hasn't had traffic in the last 7 days, the panel will be empty. Preview traffic inside the builder is not counted.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I export the data?">
    Not yet. CSV export is on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I pick a different date range?">
    Not yet. The panel shows the last 7 days. Custom ranges are on the roadmap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is visitor data anonymized?">
    Yes. Proyecta analytics do not set cookies or track users across sites. You don't need a cookie banner for analytics alone.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
